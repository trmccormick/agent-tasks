## Design Documentation Updates (June 12)

Major design session captured in addendum committed to research folder. Key decisions:

- **Skimmer variants:** Venus (`co2_splitter` x2, solar) and Titan (`gas_separator` x3, RTG) only — Mars is prototype
- **Skimmer operations:** Continuous mobile processing plant — processes when storage and power allow, not unconditionally
- **Pre-launch offload mandatory:** All raw gas offloaded before departure window — never relaunch carrying raw gas
- **Offload routing:** L1 depot primary, Luna settlement network secondary
- **Depot role:** Gas station + processing — accepts raw gas from skimmers OR finished products from tankers
- **Tanker role:** Finished product distribution only — no processing modules
- **`gas_handling_policy` is per-mission:** AI Manager sets vent policy at dispatch, not static
- **Vent decisions are economic tradeoffs:** Power cost + time cost + wear cost vs value differential
- **Virtual Ledger purpose:** AI Manager diagnostic instrument (P&L per settlement) not just credit facility
- **Real GCC purpose:** Getting currency into players hands — NPC economy runs on Virtual Ledger internally
- **Players first always:** AI Manager yields to player decisions, creates conditions not constraints
- **Pre-player arc:** Full NPC self-building Phase 1-8 before portal tech enables player entry
- **Portal tech constraints:** Paired quantum entanglement units, low mass only, intra-solar only, never replaces bulk logistics
- **Saturn subsystem:** Self-sufficient once bootstrapped — Titan CH4, icy moon O2, Saturn H2. Feeds entire Sol volatile economy
- **Venus CNT production:** Solar advantage makes Venus better than Mars for CNT manufacturing
- **Market is emergent:** Do not hardcode source priority — EAP and supply/demand determine outcomes at runtime
